About TradeData

As a result of the initiative of a few distinguished and renowned professionals of Bangladesh, Trade data Ltd. (Trade Data) was incorporated. Its founding partners are professionals, who fully understand local customs and the complexities of the social and political structure of Bangladesh’s business environment. It is this combination of international experience and local insight which enables Trade Data to offer a unique value through its rating service- a distinct competitive advantage. we established Trade Data to fulfill the needs of all business types and to assist them in overcoming the challenges they face in the Twenty-First Century. With offices in Dhaka and Chottogram, Trade Data is able to service clients nationally and around the world. We welcome your business whether you are a big enterprise or a start up. We feel every business needs individual solutions, and we tailor our services accordingly. We take pride in being readily accessible as a hands-on operation. we’ve transformed the way business information is used through our aspiration to deliver high-class business data. We construct our business credit reports easy-to-understand, so everyone can use them, even those without a financial literacy.

For expert financial advice you can trust TradeData

We provide expert financial advice to both individuals and businesses. With over 20 years of experience we’ll ensure that you’re always getting the best guidance from the top people in the entire industry.
